The story behind PRATAKE

In 2023, Eshita Dutta, a compassionate and determined young woman, founded PARTAKE, an organization dedicated to combating child hunger. Eshita's upbringing in a modest family gave her firsthand experience of the hardships faced by the less fortunate. Witnessing the plight of underprivileged children in her country, she felt a deep sense of responsibility to make a change. Driven by empathy and a desire to make a difference, Eshita established PARTAKE with a clear mission: to donate food to impoverished children and ensure they had access to nutritious meals.


With the support of volunteers, local businesses, and community members, the organization quickly took shape. PARTAKE's efforts have since touched the lives of countless children, nourishing their bodies and igniting hope for a brighter future. Eshita's inspiring leadership and the collective passion of PARTAKE's team have made it a beacon of compassion and care in the fight against child hunger.


As the years passed, PRATAKE's impact grew exponentially. Through Eshita Dutta's vision and the collective efforts of the organization, they not only nourished the bodies of countless impoverished children but also nourished their dreams, offering them a chance to escape the grip of poverty and reach for a better tomorrow. PRATAKE became a beacon of hope for children in need, lighting the path towards a more equitable and compassionate society.

VISION

PRATAKE envisions a world where hunger is eradicated, and every child's basic nutritional needs are met. Our food donation organization is driven by the belief that no child should suffer from hunger and malnutrition, regardless of their socioeconomic background. We aspire to create a society where every child has access to nutritious and wholesome food, laying the foundation for a healthier and brighter future.

Through our unwavering commitment to serve humanity, we aim to bridge the gap between abundance and scarcity by collecting surplus food from various sources and redistributing it to those in need. Our vision goes beyond short-term relief; we strive to create sustainable solutions by advocating for policies that address food insecurity at its root.

PRATAKE is not only focused on providing nourishment but also on empowering communities to become self-sufficient. We collaborate with local partners to implement educational programs, vocational training, and initiatives that enhance agricultural practices and food production.

As an organization, we envision fostering a sense of compassion and empathy within society, where people come together to support one another. We envision a world where people from all walks of life actively participate in our mission, contributing their time, resources, and expertise to uplift the lives of underprivileged children.

PRATAKE's ultimate vision is to see every child grow up healthy, well-nourished, and full of potential. We dream of a world where hunger is a thing of the past, and every child's fundamental right to food is upheld. Together, we can build a brighter and more equitable future for the generations to come.
